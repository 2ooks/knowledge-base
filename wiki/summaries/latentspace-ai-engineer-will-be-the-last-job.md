# [AINews] AI Engineer Will Be the LAST Job

**Source URL**: https://www.latent.space/p/ainews-ai-engineer-will-be-the-last
**Date**: March 7, 2026
**Publication**: Latent Space (swyx)

## Summary

swyx makes the provocative case that AI engineering is the last job standing, not just via Jevons Paradox but because all agents are converging to coding agents with extra skills -- Code Mode/CLIs are eating MCP, filesystems are eating Memory/RAG, and sandboxes are eating Vision. Software engineering now accounts for 50%+ of Claude model usage and is trending toward 80-90%. The piece also covers GPT-5.4's launch (tied #1 on Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index with Gemini 3.1 Pro), Claude Opus 4.6 finding 22 Firefox vulnerabilities in 2 weeks, and the emergence of agent-native CI pipelines. For BD, the central thesis is that the platform that wins coding agent infrastructure wins all agent infrastructure.

## Key Claims

- Software engineering represents 50%+ of Claude model usage and is trending toward 80-90%, not plateauing.
- All agents are converging to "coding agents with extra skills" -- Code Mode/CLIs eating MCP, filesystems eating Memory/RAG, sandboxes eating Vision.
- Software engineer job postings are rebounding higher even as overall job postings trend down (per Citadel's response to Citrini Research).
- GPT-5.4 (xhigh) tied Gemini 3.1 Pro Preview at #1 on Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index (score 57), priced at $2.50/$15 per 1M input/output tokens.
- GPT-5.4 Pro shows a +10 point jump on CritPt physics reasoning but at $180/1M output tokens, with benchmark run costs exceeding $1K.
- Claude Opus 4.6 found 22 confirmed vulnerabilities in Firefox in 2 weeks (14 high-severity), representing ~20% of Mozilla's 2025 high-severity fixes.
- Factory AI claims each PR runs 40+ CI checks in <6 minutes, enabling "merge recklessly" as a development posture.
- vLLM v0.17 ships FlashAttention 4, cross-platform Triton attention backend, and support for Qwen3.5 with GDN.
