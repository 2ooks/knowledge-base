# AI is Eating Search

**Source URL**: https://www.latent.space/p/ai-is-eating-search
**Date**: July 23, 2025
**Publication**: Latent Space

## Summary

Interview with Robert McCloy of Scrunch AI on how AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity) is restructuring the discovery and conversion funnel. ChatGPT handles 2.5B prompts/day and is on track to match Google's daily search volume by end of 2026. AI agents do not browse like humans -- they need queryable, chunkable data, spawning a new optimization discipline (variously called AI SEO or GEO). Businesses report 2-4x higher conversion rates from AI-referred visitors compared to traditional search, signaling a fundamental shift in how content must be structured for agent consumption.

## Key Claims

- ChatGPT processes 2.5 billion prompts per day and is projected to match Google's daily search volume by end of 2026.
- Visitors arriving via AI search engines convert at 2-4x the rate of traditional search visitors.
- A new optimization discipline is emerging -- AI SEO / GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) -- focused on making content queryable and chunkable for agents.
- AI browsers and agents consume content fundamentally differently than humans, requiring structural changes in content delivery (e.g., LMS.txt, programmatic SEO).
- Prompt injection is a real risk vector in AI search optimization.
- Scrunch AI helps brands rewrite content on-the-fly based on what agents are looking for, indicating real commercial demand for this category.
