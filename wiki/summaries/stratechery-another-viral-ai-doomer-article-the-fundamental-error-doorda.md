# Another Viral AI Doomer Article, The Fundamental Error, DoorDash's AI Advantages

**Source URL**: https://stratechery.com/2026/another-viral-ai-doomer-article-the-fundamental-error-doordashs-ai-advantages/
**Date**: February 24, 2026
**Publication**: Stratechery

## Summary

Thompson dismantles a viral Citrini Research report that triggered a broad AI scare trade (IBM -13%, DoorDash -6%+), arguing the report's core error is a static view of the economy that assumes incumbents are rent extractors rather than value creators. Using DoorDash as a case study, Thompson demonstrates why physical-world, multi-sided marketplace businesses are uniquely defensible against AI disruption: three-sided network effects, exclusive data, physical-world interaction, high-risk payment workflows, and pre-existing transaction-based business models. The real estate agent example from the report actually proves the opposite of its thesis -- agents survived full information transparency from the internet.

## Key Claims

- Citrini Research report triggered IBM -13% (worst since 2000), DoorDash -6%, Uber -4%, Visa/Mastercard -4%+ in a single day.
- The report's thesis: a "rent-extraction layer" built on human limitations would be destroyed by AI agents.
- Real estate commissions have compressed only due to regulatory (court) action, not technology -- despite full information availability online for years.
- DoorDash's three-sided network effects (customers, restaurants, drivers) are significantly harder to displace than two-sided or single-sided markets.
- Physical-world interaction "cubes" the difficulty of vibe-coding a replacement for DoorDash.
- DoorDash's transaction-based monetization means it collects revenue whether initiated by an agent or a human.
- The fundamental analytical error: assuming the economy is static and incumbent success comes from extraction rather than value creation.
