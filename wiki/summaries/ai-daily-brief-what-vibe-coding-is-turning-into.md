# What Vibe Coding Is Turning Into

**Source URL**: https://aidailybrief.beehiiv.com/p/what-vibe-coding-is-turning-into
**Date**: March 12, 2026
**Publication**: AI Daily Brief

## Summary

Tracks vibe coding's evolution from a coding-specific practice into a general knowledge work paradigm, driven by Replit Agent 4 and Perplexity Computer for Enterprise. Replit expanded from coding platform to integrated productivity suite with collaborative canvas, parallel execution, and multiplayer mode. Perplexity pivoted from search to an AI "everything machine" with 400+ enterprise app integrations and usage-based pricing. Four emerging patterns define the trajectory: blended canvases, persistent context, multi-agent systems, and multiplayer collaboration. Karpathy's framing: "We're going to need a bigger IDE."

## Key Claims

- Lovable added $100M ARR in a single month, jumping from $300M to $400M ARR; Cursor doubled to $2B ARR over three months.
- Ramp and Stripe both launched virtual payment cards specifically for AI agents, with spend limits and real-time monitoring.
- Ramp AI Index: Anthropic at 24.4% vs. OpenAI at 34.4% and falling; Anthropic winning 70% of first-time AI business head-to-head.
- Perplexity Computer for Enterprise has 400+ app integrations; charges on usage rather than seats.
- Replit Agent 4 adds collaborative canvas, parallel execution, and multiplayer mode — Paul Graham called it a "genuine paradigm shift."
- Netflix could pay $600M for Ben Affleck's AI firm InterPositive — potentially largest AI media acquisition to date.
- Tesla and xAI working on "Macrohard" (Digital Optimus) project — Grok as system 2 conductor over real-time computer use.
