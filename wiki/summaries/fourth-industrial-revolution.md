# The Fourth Industrial Revolution: The Intelligence Revolution
- **Source:** https://cloudedjudgement.substack.com/p/the-fourth-industrial-revolution
- **Date:** July 17, 2024
- **Author:** Jamin Ball (Altimeter)

## Summary
Ball frames the current AI wave as the Fourth Industrial Revolution, following mechanization (~1760), electrification (~1870), and digitization (~1960). The gap between revolutions is compressing: 110 → 90 → 55 years. We are only ~5 years into this one. The piece catalogs all 11 essential data center components — servers, networking, storage, cooling, power, security, monitoring, racks, cabling, environmental controls, and software — with key vendors for each. The major unaddressed bottlenecks are real estate and power proximity.

## Key Claims
- Industrial revolutions accelerate: 110 → 90 → 55 years between each
- We are only ~5 years into the Intelligence Revolution; decades remain
- 11 data center components, each being reinvented for AI workloads
- Real estate-power proximity is a major bottleneck: power lost in transmission favors co-location
- Natural gas could play a significant role in on-site power generation
- Each component (networking, cooling, storage, etc.) represents a startup opportunity
