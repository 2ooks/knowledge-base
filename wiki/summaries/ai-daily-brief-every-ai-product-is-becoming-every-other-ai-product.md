# Every AI Product Is Becoming Every Other AI Product

**Source URL**: https://aidailybrief.beehiiv.com/p/every-ai-product-is-becoming-every-other-ai-product
**Date**: March 21, 2026
**Publication**: AI Daily Brief

## Summary

Documents the convergence of AI products across categories — Google AI Studio adding vibe coding, Lovable expanding beyond code gen into data analysis and marketing, OpenAI consolidating into a desktop superapp, and Claude Code extending via Telegram/Discord channels. The underlying thesis is that coding capability is the universal substrate for all knowledge work, and when shipping costs approach zero and switching costs are also near zero, every company becomes every other company. This creates a no-moat environment where continual pivots become the only viable strategy.

## Key Claims

- Lovable added $100M ARR in a single month (February), jumping from $300M to $400M ARR.
- Google AI Studio rebuilt with full-stack vibe coding: one-click databases, auth, payment processor connections, multiplayer support.
- OpenAI plans to combine ChatGPT, Codex, and Atlas browser into a single desktop superapp.
- Claude Code now controllable via Telegram (1B MAU) and Discord — Anthropic extending via ecosystem rather than consolidating.
- 62% of AI Daily Brief audience had use cases extending beyond pure assistant into automated or agentic territory in February 2026.
- Bezos raising a $100B fund (Project Prometheus) to buy industrial companies and transform them with AI.
- Apple blocking Replit and Vibecode app updates under rules prohibiting apps that run code changing app functionality.
