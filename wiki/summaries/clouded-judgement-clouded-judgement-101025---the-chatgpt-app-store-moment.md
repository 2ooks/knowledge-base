# Clouded Judgement 10.10.25 - The ChatGPT App Store Moment

**Source URL**: https://cloudedjudgement.substack.com/p/clouded-judgement-101025-the-chatgpt
**Date**: October 10, 2025
**Publication**: Clouded Judgement (Jamin Ball / Altimeter)

## Summary

Analyzes OpenAI's launch of native apps inside ChatGPT and the Apps SDK as a platform-defining moment analogous to the early iPhone App Store. Ball argues ChatGPT is transitioning from reasoning engine to internet interface — a super-app where users call services by name (Spotify, DoorDash, Expedia) and ChatGPT handles interactions inline. The deeper thesis is that OpenAI is building not just a model company but an "AI cloud hyperscaler" — infrastructure for deploying reasoning rather than code — with the app ecosystem as the distribution layer on top.

## Key Claims

- OpenAI launched "apps" inside ChatGPT with an Apps SDK for developers; apps callable by name in conversational flow.
- Apps use Model Context Protocol and exposed primitives (context, API calls) — integrations, not yet native experiences.
- Ball draws parallel to early mobile web apps before Apple opened deeper APIs — predicts evolution toward true native ChatGPT apps with persistence and interactivity.
- Launch partners include DoorDash, Uber, Spotify, Figma, Canva, Expedia.
- OpenAI building massive data centers and custom compute — Ball argues they are scaling an AI cloud, not just serving inference.
- SaaS median overall multiple: 5.1x; high growth median: 30.2x; median NTM growth rate: 12%.
- Median SaaS FCF margin: 18%; median net retention: 108%.
