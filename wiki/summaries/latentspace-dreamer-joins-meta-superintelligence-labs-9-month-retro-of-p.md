# AINews: Dreamer Joins Meta Superintelligence Labs — 9 Month Retro of Personal Superintelligence

- **Source URL**: https://www.latent.space/p/ainews-dreamer-joins-meta-superintelligence
- **Date**: March 24, 2026
- **Publication**: Latent Space

## Summary

Meta Superintelligence Labs (led by Nat Friedman and Alex) execuhired Dreamer just 11 days after recording a podcast with Latent Space, following Meta's $2B Manus acquisition in December 2025 — combining the two teams into one of the most formidable consumer agent labs on Earth. This marks the 9-month anniversary of Zuckerberg's "Personal Superintelligence" manifesto, which frames the endgame as everyone having a personal AI that knows them deeply and helps achieve their goals. The broader AINews roundup covers Anthropic pushing Claude computer use onto the macOS desktop (mouse, keyboard, screen control), the agent stack converging on long-running parallel tool-rich workflows (Hermes Agent, T3 Code, Command Center), and operational fragility — not model intelligence — becoming the real bottleneck, with practitioners noting that superficial parallelization often becomes "slop theater" rather than real throughput.

## Key Claims

- Meta Superintelligence Labs execuhired Dreamer within 11 days of the Latent Space podcast recording
- Meta acquired Manus for $2B in December 2025, also completed in approximately 10 days
- Combining Dreamer and Manus teams creates one of the most formidable consumer agent labs globally
- Anthropic launched Claude computer use on macOS: mouse, keyboard, and screen control via Claude Cowork and Claude Code
- Hyperagents/DGM-H enables agents to improve not only task behavior but the meta-procedure that generates future improvements, transferring across coding, paper review, robotics, and Olympiad grading
- WebArena-Infinity reduces browser environment construction cost from months of labor to under 10 hours and less than $100 per environment
- LeWorldModel claims stable end-to-end JEPA training from pixels with 15M params, 1 GPU, and ~48-50x planning speedups
- MiniMax launched a flat-rate Token Plan covering text, speech, music, video, and image APIs under one subscription
