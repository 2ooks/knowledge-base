# Executive Briefing: Your 2026 AI Roadmap in Minutes (5 Drivers, Cross-Cuts, Next Steps)

**Source URL**: https://natesnewsletter.substack.com/p/executive-briefing-the-5-ai-shifts
**Date**: September 28, 2025
**Publication**: Nate's Newsletter (Substack)

## Summary

A strategic planning guide for Q4 2025 / 2026 AI roadmapping, identifying five key inflection points and cross-cutting patterns that should inform budget allocation and build decisions. The core argument is that AI is moving faster than annual planning cycles can accommodate, and that complexity-protected industries are losing their defenses as vertical expertise that took years to build can now be replicated in months. The window to build new moats through memory systems, compliance infrastructure, and vertical depth is narrow and closing.

## Key Claims

- AI is moving faster than annual planning cycles; 2026 budgets will be locked in 8 weeks but the competitive landscape will shift multiple times before execution.
- Complexity-protected industries are losing their defenses -- vertical expertise built over years can be replicated by AI in months.
- Five inflection points are identified for 2026 planning: compliance, memory, outcomes-based metrics, on-device AI, and premium/enterprise AI tiers.
- The moats that protected margins in 2024 will not hold in 2026 unless companies understand where defensibility now resides.
- New moats come from memory systems, compliance infrastructure, and vertical depth -- and the window to build them is closing.
- Teams that distinguish compounding advantages from table stakes will make fundamentally different allocation decisions than those operating on vibes and vendor pitches.
